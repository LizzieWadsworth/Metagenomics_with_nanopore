The MultiQC Graphs

The graphs for MultiQC are similar to those for FastQC but are combined for you reads. While we have paired reads, this will combine all forward reads and all reverse reads. We’ll do this again after we’ve merged the reads, which will give us one value for every sample.

General Statistics

MultiQC gives you a useful overview of your samples:

Sample Name Dups GC Seqs
forward 98.1% 54.0% 0.1M
reverse 98.1% 53.0% 0.1M

Sequence counts

A count of unique and duplicate reads. Because we’re looking at a PCR amplified genes, we expect lots of duplicates!

FastQC: Mean Quality Scores

Mean quality scores for the reads.
